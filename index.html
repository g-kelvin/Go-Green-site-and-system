    OUR COMPANY

    Go Green By Tapping Systems LTD is a company dedicated to harnessing renewable energy sources. John Mumbu Njuguna founded the company in 2016 with the aim of tapping into green energy sources. The company has grown rapidly over the years and is now one of the leading renewable energy companies in the country. The company is committed to providing its clients with clean and sustainable energy solutions. Go Green By Tapping Systems LTD has highly skilled and experienced professionals dedicated to providing the best possible service to their clients. The company is committed to providing its clients with the best possible renewable energy solutions and is always looking for new and innovative ways to improve its services. The company was later registered and subsequently incorporated in the year 2019. We Offer;
