e-NSC Cast iron end suction pumps

Taking end suction pumps to a new level
The Lowara e-NSC end suction pumps combine high efficiency with high flexibility regarding installation, material options and temperature. This make the e-NSC the natural choice for water transport, hydronic heating and chiller systems, fire protection systems and a vast number of industrial applications. HIGH EFFICIENCY
New designed high efficiency hydraulics well exceeding the ErP2015 level and IE3 motors set the basis for very low operation costs. To optimize the operation costs further, the e-NSC can be equipped with a Hydrovar variable speed pump controller (up to 45 kW). In installations with a big variance in the demand, it can save up to 70% of the energy cost compared to a fixed speed pump.

Specifications

1 Delivery up to 1800 m³/h
2 Head up to 160 m
3 Power supply three-phase 50 and 60 Hz
4 Power from 0,25 kW up to 355 kW
5 Maximum operating pressure 16 bar
5 Temperature of pumped liquid from -25°C to +140°C
